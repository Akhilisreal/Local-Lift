Help & Support

Welcome! Here are some quick pointers to get the most out of Local Lift.

Account

  • To log in or sign up, use the links on the login/signup pages.
  • If you need to log out, use the "Logout" button in the header.

Using the app

  • Browse businesses from the home page and filter by category or sort by rating.
  • Add favorites using the favorite button on a business cards; view them on the Favorites page.
  • Open a business to see details and reviews on the Business page.
